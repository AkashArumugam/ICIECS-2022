Second International Conference on Innovations in Electronics and Communication Systems

(ICIECS 2022)


On Nov 24, 25 & 26, 2022

Organized By
Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

About the Conference

The primary objective of the conference is to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge developments and innovations in the fields of Electronics and communication systems. This forum facilitate faculty members, researchers, students and industry people presenting their research outcomes and defending, expanding, or changing them in response to questions, criticism, and other feedback from the participants. The conference assist to establish relationships among the researchers and innovators in the fields of Electronics and communication systems.
