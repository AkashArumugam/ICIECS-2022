About the Conference

The primary objective of the conference is to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge development innovators in the fields of Electronics and communication systems. This forum facilitate faculty, researchers, students and industry people presenting their research outcomes and defending, expanding, or changing them in response to questions, criticism, and other feedback from the participants. The conference provide a platform for relationships among the researchers and innovators in the fields of Electronics and communication systems.
